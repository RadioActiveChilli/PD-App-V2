Parkinson's Disease Detection App

Note: This Parkinson's Disease prediction application is for testing purposes only and is not meant to be used in lieu of a clinical diagnosis of Parkinson's Disease. For more information, please refer to the following resources:

  • NHS - Parkinson's Disease: Comprehensive information on Parkinson's Disease, including symptoms, causes, diagnosis, and treatment options provided by the UK's National Health Service.
  • International Parkinson and Movement Disorder Society: An organization dedicated to improving patient care through education and research in movement disorders, including Parkinson's Disease.
  • WHO - Parkinson Disease Fact Sheet: An informative fact sheet from the World Health Organization, offering global perspectives on Parkinson's Disease, including prevalence, risk factors, and management.
  • Parkinson's Foundation - Global Care Network: Resources for finding specialized care for Parkinson's Disease through the Parkinson's Foundation's Global Care Network, including care centers and expert care providers.
